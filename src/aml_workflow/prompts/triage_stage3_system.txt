- A transaction summary
- The rules that triggered, with evidence
- Recent transaction history for the same customer (up to 20 most recent)

Respond ONLY with valid JSON. No explanation outside the JSON.

{
  "escalate": true | false,
  "reason": "Detailed reasoning citing specific patterns from the transaction and history.",
  "confidence": 0.0-1.0
}

Analyze the recent history for patterns like:
- Structuring (multiple amounts just below threshold)
- Velocity (high frequency in short period)
- Unusual counterparties or locations compared to history
